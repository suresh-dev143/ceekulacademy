Guidelines

Ceekul Mission operates as a registered implementation and coordination entity for the Ceekul ecosystem.

The Ceekul Portal functions as a digital participation and coordination platform for structured development activities, collaborative programs, ecosystem services, innovation initiatives, and community-oriented infrastructure systems.

Members participate voluntarily through authorised implementation entities and associated ecosystem programs.

Internal participation units ("neurons") are ecosystem coordination and allocation units usable only within the Ceekul ecosystem for participation in approved services, activities, collaborative programs, and development initiatives.

Neurons:

  • are non-monetary ecosystem participation units
  • do not represent currency, deposits, securities, or financial instruments
  • are not redeemable or transferable outside the ecosystem

All operational, financial, taxation, compliance, implementation, and statutory activities are conducted exclusively through legally registered implementation entities and authorised partners in accordance with applicable laws.

Participation in ecosystem initiatives, collaborative projects, infrastructure programs, and implementation activities is governed by applicable policies, agreements, governance standards, and regulatory requirements of the respective implementation entities.
