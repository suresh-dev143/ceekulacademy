Pathway to Membership

Reformer membership is not granted — it is demonstrated. There is no application form. The pathway below is the selection process.

01
Submit a Reform Analysis

Document a real institution: its current architecture, the outcomes it actually produces, and a structural alternative. One page. Evidence-based. Submitted through the Ceekul platform.

02
Complete the REF Protocol Assessment

A structured assessment testing systemic analysis, leverage point identification, and institutional redesign methodology. Passing score required: 80/100.

03
Run a Live Reform Sprint

30 days embedded in a Ceekul community. Produce one deployable policy prototype. The sprint is reviewed by existing Reformers before membership is confirmed.

Begin with Reform Theory → Start in Ceekul Academy — Reform Theory is prerequisite coursework

Reformers hold no attachment to the past and no fear of the friction of change. They operate with one criterion: does this system, as currently designed, produce the civilizational outcomes we have committed to? If not, it is redesigned — not defended.
