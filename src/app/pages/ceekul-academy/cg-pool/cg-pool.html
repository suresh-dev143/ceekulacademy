Legacy Distribution Protocol

The process begins only when the deceased member's information is filed by their spouse, any biological child, or either parent — each through their own CB ID. Once the algorithm verifies the filer's eligibility, the member's CB ID initiates distribution automatically — first splitting MY Neurons into the member's own FUN, CUN, and SUN streams, then transferring those balances to the registered spouse. If the spouse has also passed, the balances flow to biological children. If neither is available, everything flows to CG100000000000.

Any investment returns that arrive after the member's passing continue to accumulate in the member's CB ID. Every 6 months, the same protocol activates on the accumulated balance — following the same spouse → children → CG100000000000 priority chain.
