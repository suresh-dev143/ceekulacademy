Ideas & Opinions

Social and Political Views

Einstein explores the rise of nationalism after World War I and advocates for pacifism, disarmament, and international cooperation. He stresses individual moral freedom and the responsibility to resist evil orders.

Science and Philosophy

The collection traces the evolution of physics from Newtonian mechanics to General Relativity. He discusses the relationship between science and religion, suggesting they do not conflict when they respect each other's boundaries—religion focusing on moral action and science on facts.

Human Rights and Education

Einstein emphasizes that everyone should be free to develop intellectually and artistically, viewing education as a tool for understanding facts rather than just memorising them.
