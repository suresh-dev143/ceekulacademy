How Your Ads Deliver

Every active campaign is pre-scheduled before each session starts. When the clock hits the 50-minute mark, matching ads fire in O(1) time — no runtime computation needed.

◻

1. Upload & Commit

Your media is committed to UCE and receives a permanent Content ID. This anchors your ad in the delivery ledger.

→
◈

2. Index & Filter

Your criteria — category, age group, tags, bid rate — are indexed. Sessions that don't match are excluded instantly at schedule time.

→
⧖

3. Pre-Schedule

Before each session starts, a greedy 600-second ad pack is assembled from the highest-bidding matching campaigns.

→
◎

4. Auto-Deliver

At the 50-minute mark the pre-built plan fires instantly to every matching student. Pure O(1) — no real-time matching ever runs.
