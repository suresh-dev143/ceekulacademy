DIRECTORS

The primary goal of any Director is to

1

Organize regular seminars and workshops on the automation of services and infrastructure on the chosen area.

2

Explore the technological solutions to the problems raised by the Ceebrain members with the Vice Chancellor.

3

Discuss ways to implement the plans finalized by the Executive Council during regular online or offline meetings of the respective assembly.

There are 2 types of Directors: (1) Director of a particular discipline at a particular Regional Ceekul Center (2) Director of Local Ceekul Center to guide all the disciplines.
